About Us

ARISE Initiative is a youth-led project based in Gitega, Burundi. We empower high school students across Burundi with technology and critical thinking skills — including students with disabilities — because the future belongs to all of them.

Our Programmes

  • Debate & Advocacy (Active) — Organized interschool debate championship, trained 120+ students, and built permanent clubs in 4 schools.
  • Tech & Digital Literacy (Upcoming) — Preparing youth with essential digital skills for the modern world.

What We Stand For

BURUNDI: "IGIHUGU CIFASHE KANDI GITEYE IMBERE."

ARISE is more than a name — it defines our values:

  • A: Advocacy. Championing voices and driving meaningful change
  • R: Respect. Honoring dignity, the foundation of unity
  • I: Innovation. Bold ideas lighting the path forward
  • S: Speaking. Words that educate, mobilize, and matter
  • E: Empowerment for Development, equipping citizens to shape their nation’s destiny
